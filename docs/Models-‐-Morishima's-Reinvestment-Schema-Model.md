This models Morishima's 'correction' of Marx's reproduction schema from Volume II of Capital. A basic rundown is that we have two departments, a capital goods department. and a labor goods department. (You can assume that they are aggregated in an approximation, aggregated perfectly in the case of all dept. sectors having equal compositions of capital, or not aggregated at all and that we are just dealing with a two commodity economy, it makes no difference).
